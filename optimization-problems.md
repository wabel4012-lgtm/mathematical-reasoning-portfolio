## 2. Linear Optimization Example

Maximize:
P = 3x + 2y

Subject to:
x + y ≤ 10  
x ≥ 0, y ≥ 0

### Solution Strategy
Evaluate corner points of feasible region.

Optimization problems require balancing constraints while maximizing or minimizing objective functions.
